MIMS Ad Manager: A Modern Ad Serving Solution

MIMS Ad Manager is a proof-of-concept advertising platform that demonstrates key ad serving capabilities including targeting, frequency capping, and viewability tracking.

The platform supports multiple ad formats across web and mobile platforms, with real-time reporting and campaign management through an intuitive dashboard.

Key features include:

  • Key-value targeting
  • Per-day frequency capping
  • Viewability tracking (50%/1s standard)
  • Cross-platform SDKs
